Overview

Partner Programme

Our Franchise Partner Programme offers entrepreneurs an opportunity to leverage the power of the RINL Steel brand and a proven business model rooted in long-term value creation.

  • Selected franchise partners meet minimum capacity standards with sound financial and governance credentials.
  • Partners manufacture, market, and sell RINL-branded products through an authorised partner network.
  • RINL provides technical, quality, marketing, and relationship support through the partner journey.
